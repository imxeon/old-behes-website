WILDCARD ENTRIES

If you failed to qualify or attend a regional round near you this season; but think you deserve a spot at the finals, all you need to do is convince us that you deserve to make it! You can do so by writing a short essay outlining what your team would bring to the tournament, and why you deserve a spot among India's best. The top ten entries will win wildcard entries to the finals.

BEHES SPIRIT AWARD

The Behes Spirit Award is meant to recognize debaters who have helped spread the Behes message of free speech and reasoned expression among their peers and helped set up a community of debaters at their schools. Please vote for someone who will be at the Maha Behes 2017 as a participant.
